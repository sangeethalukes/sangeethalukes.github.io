Operative Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Our expertise lies in the rehabilitation of orthopaedic surgical conditions. We cater to a wide range of surgeries, including total knee and hip replacements, meniscal and ACL tears, as well as fractures of the ankle, shoulder, forearm, elbow, and wrist. Our rehabilitation programs are meticulously designed to enhance range of motion, strength, mobility, and psychological well-being. Our ultimate goal is to assist patients in regaining their prior level of function and confidence, enabling them to return to their peak potential and regain independence in their everyday activities.

Available Rehabilitation Services:

  • Comprehensive Post-Operative Care: We offer specialised rehabilitation services tailored to a variety of orthopaedic post-operative needs.
  • Fracture Rehabilitation: Our program includes focused rehabilitation for:
    • Clavicle fracture
    • Shoulder fracture
    • Elbow fracture
    • Wrist fracture
    • Pelvis fracture
    • Patellar fracture
    • Knee and ankle fractures
  • Tailored Rehabilitation for Complex Cases: We provide an integrated post-operative rehabilitation approach for patients with multiple fractures. This is particularly relevant for those recovering from spinal fractures or undergoing surgical spinal cord decompression following a road traffic accident.

Our rehabilitation approach is holistic and patient-centered, focusing on both physical recovery and emotional support to facilitate a smooth and effective return to daily life.
