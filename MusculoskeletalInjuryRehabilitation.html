Musculoskeletal Injury Rehabilitation

When you come to us with musculoskeletal issues, expect a thorough assessment designed to diagnose and address your specific injury. Our approach involves a detailed understanding of how the injury occurred, allowing us to offer targeted treatments tailored just for you. We believe in a holistic evaluation, considering both your physical and mental well-being as part of the recovery process.

Treatments We Offer:

  • Arthritis Management: We offer specialised care for arthritis affecting the shoulder, knees, and ankles. Our goal is to reduce pain and improve your quality of life.
  • Rotator Cuff and Shoulder Conditions: Our expertise includes treatment for rotator cuff injuries and shoulder muscle strains. Each treatment plan is customised to meet your unique needs.
  • Spinal Postural Correction: Correcting spinal alignment is a central aspect of our rehabilitation efforts, aiming to relieve pain and improve posture.
  • Comprehensive Shoulder Care: We address a variety of shoulder conditions including impingement, rotator cuff tears, frozen shoulder, tennis elbow, and golfer's elbow, focusing on restoring function and reducing discomfort.
  • Tendonitis and Bursitis Management: Our protocols are designed to effectively treat conditions like tendonitis and bursitis, facilitating a return to functional activities.
  • Groin, Muscle, and Hamstring Rehabilitation: We provide treatments for groin and muscle strains, as well as hamstring tightness, to promote recovery and enhance athletic performance.
  • Ankle and Joint Sprain Rehabilitation: Specialised care for ankle sprains, other joint sprains, and plantar fasciitis is available to improve mobility and alleviate pain.
  • Pain Management and Functional Recovery: Tailored treatment plans are available for conditions like patellar tendonitis, neck, low back, and mid-back pain, aimed at relieving pain and restoring functionality.
  • Wrist and Hand Care: We offer focused treatments for wrist strains, carpal tunnel syndrome, and de Quervain's disease to help regain function.
  • Sports Massage Therapy: As part of our comprehensive care, sports massage therapy is available to assist in alleviating muscle spasms and pain, contributing to overall muscle recovery.

Each of our treatment plans is designed with your specific needs in mind, ensuring a personalized approach to your recovery.
